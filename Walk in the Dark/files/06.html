The real barrier for blind and visually impaired people isn’t blindness—it’s society’s inaccessibility and misconceptions. We must design a world that values inclusion, adopts assistive technologies, and breaks down biases.

Sighted individuals can listen, advocate, and educate. For blind individuals, independence lies in access, community, and courage.

Together, we can create a world where vision is far more than what we see.
